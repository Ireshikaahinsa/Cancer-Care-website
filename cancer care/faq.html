Donation FAQS





Monthly Giving Questions

Can I make monthly, automatic donations to the Cancer Care Organization?

* Use our safe and secure online form to join the fight against cancer today

* Talk with someone about a monthly donation, call +94112-342390

* For all other donation or general questions, call +94112-342391

* To request a monthly giving form, email donorservices@cancercare.org


Why should I donate monthly?

Monthly giving to The Cancer Care Organization is simply the best cancer-fighting investment that you can make - it's the most effective and manageable way you can support our efforts to minimize suffering from cancer. Your support funds lifesaving research and provides essential programs for prevention and early detection, patient support, and the trusted information cancer patients and their families need.


When will my credit card first be charged?

All monthly charges occur on the 15th of each month. Your first online gift won't be charged to your card until the 15th. All subsequent charges will occur on the 15th of each month.


Once I've made an online gift, how can I make changes to my account, i.e. change amount, change my credit card number?

It's so easy! Call us at +94112-342390 or email us at donorservices@cancercare.org.

For all other donation or general questions, call +94112-342391.


What if I need to stop giving? Can I stop whenever I want?

Yes, just let us know either by calling us at +94-112-342390 or emailing us at donorservices@cancercare.org.

For all other donation or general questions, call +94112-342391.

Memorial Giving Questions

How do I make a donation in honor or memory of someone?

It's easy to celebrate the life of a friend or loved one by making a donation to the Cancer Care Organization in their honor or memory. Simply complete the information in the Gift Type portion of the donation form.


Is the family notified of my donation amount?

No. The amount of money donated through memorial or honor donations is kept confidential.


Do you have family addresses on file?

Due to privacy laws, we are not able to keep addresses on file.
