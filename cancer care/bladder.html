Bladder Cancer

What is known as Bladder?

The bladder is an broad ,organ that stores urine before it leaves the body during urination.This makes Bladder as an important organ to a human.The urinary tract is made up of the kidneys, ureters, and urethra.The ureter is a main tube that outs from each kidney into the bladder. The urethra is the tube that transfers urine out of the body. The bladder, like other parts of the urinary tract, is lined with a layer of cells called the urothelium. This layer of cells is separated from muscularis propria. muscularis propria is the bladder wall muscles and lamina propria a thin, fibrous band. Smoking is a major concern for Bladder Cancer. We can diagnosed this cancer type in early stages.

What is Bladder Cancer

Bladder cancer begins when healthy cells in the bladder lining, mainly urothelial cells, differ and advance over control, aligning a mass called a tumor. Urothelial cells also line the renal pelvis and ureters. Even though cancer that increases in the renal pelvis and ureters is treated a kind of kidney cancer. It is often treated in the same way as bladder cancer. A tumor can be cancerous or benevolent. A cancerous tumor is malignant, which it can spread to other parts of the body. A benign tumor, Can grow but will not spread. Benign bladder tumors are rare in this case.
